# Product Context: Endurain

## Why Endurain Exists

Endurain addresses the need for a fitness tracking solution where users retain full control over their data and hosting. Unlike commercial platforms like Strava, Endurain prioritizes privacy and data ownership.

## Problems Solved

*   **Data Privacy Concerns:** Users are increasingly wary of how large platforms use their personal fitness data. Endurain offers a self-hosted alternative.
*   **Vendor Lock-in:** Commercial platforms can change features, pricing, or terms of service. Endurain provides stability and user control.
*   **Lack of Customization:** Self-hosting allows for potential customization and integration possibilities beyond what commercial platforms offer.

## How It Should Work

*   Users should be able to easily record, upload (manually or via integrations), and view their fitness activities (runs, rides, swims, etc.).
*   The interface should provide clear visualizations and summaries of activity data (distance, duration, pace, elevation, calories, maps, etc.).
*   Data synchronization with services like Strava and Garmin Connect should be seamless.
*   Users should be able to manage their profile, gear, and settings.
*   The system should be reliable and performant.

## User Experience Goals

*   **Intuitive:** Easy for new users to understand and navigate.
*   **Informative:** Provide meaningful insights into fitness progress.
*   **Private:** Users should feel confident their data is secure and under their control.
*   **Reliable:** The application should function consistently.
*   **Integrated:** Seamlessly connect with other services where desired by the user.
